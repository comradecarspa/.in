About Us

At Comrades CarSpa, we believe your car deserves the best care. Our team of expert detailers uses premium products and techniques to restore and protect your vehicle's beauty and value.

With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in comprehensive detailing services that go beyond the surface, ensuring your car looks and feels brand new.

Our Services

Interior Detailing

Our interior detailing process involves thorough vacuuming, shampooing carpets and upholstery, and sanitizing all surfaces to create a fresh, clean environment inside your vehicle.

  • Improved air quality and reduced allergens
  • Removal of stubborn stains and odors
  • Enhanced resale value of your vehicle
  • Increased comfort for daily driving
  • Better safety with clear, unobstructed views

Exterior Detailing

We start with a gentle hand wash, followed by clay treatment to remove contaminants, polishing to restore shine, and waxing for protection against the elements.

  • Long-lasting protection from environmental damage
  • Restored paint shine and clarity
  • Removal of embedded contaminants
  • Extended paint longevity
  • Eye-catching, showroom-quality appearance

Teflon Coating

Teflon coating applies a thin polymer layer that creates a protective barrier, making it easier to clean and maintain your vehicle's surfaces.

  • Excellent water beading for quick drying
  • Ease of cleaning and maintenance
  • Short-term protection against minor scratches
  • Cost-effective coating option
  • Enhanced gloss and shine

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating uses SiO2 nanotechnology to bond with your paint, creating a durable, glass-like layer that protects against chemicals, UV rays, and minor abrasions.

  • Superior durability up to 2-5 years
  • Resistance to chemical stains and etching
  • UV protection to prevent fading
  • Extreme hydrophobicity for water repellency
  • Enhanced depth and gloss

Graphene Coating

Graphene coating leverages the strength of graphene for superior protection, offering better thermal conductivity and slickness compared to traditional ceramic coatings.

  • Excellent thermal conductivity for heat dissipation
  • Ultra-slick surface for superior water beading
  • Enhanced durability and longevity
  • Improved hydrophobicity and self-cleaning
  • Advanced protection against environmental factors

PPF (Paint Protection Film)

Paint Protection Film is a transparent urethane film that acts as a physical barrier, protecting your paint from stone chips, scratches, and other road debris.

  • Stone chip and scratch protection
  • Self-healing properties on some films
  • Invisible defense that maintains appearance
  • Preserves original paint condition
  • Easy removal without damaging paint
